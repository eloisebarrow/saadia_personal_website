Experience

  • Department of Education (2013-present)

    • Classroom interpreting in subjects such as Math, History, Government, English and Science. Additional interpreting on school trips to various settings such as the Math Museum, Bronx Zoo, The Statue of Liberty/Ellis Island among others.

  • Sorenson Video Relay Service (2012-2015)

    • As a Video Relay Interpreter I interpreted phone calls between hearing and deaf individuals discussing a variety of topics including education, medical, business, personal and many others.

  • TED Conference (2013)

    • Acted as one of three interpreters for a deaf TED Fellow artist. This included pre-conference workshops, tours and socializing with other Fellows. During the weeklong conference, interpreted many Talks by professionals in fields varying from nuclear engineering, photography, linguistics and many more.

  • Pro-Bono Work (2012-present)

    • Continuously giving back to the deaf community and expanding my range and comfort in different settings such as theater (NYDT, SensesAskew) and various workshops/meetings.
